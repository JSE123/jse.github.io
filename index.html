Spaguetti Bolognaise

Ingredients:

1 Onion 1 Carrots
2 rachers bacon 1 x 400g tin tomatoes
500g mince 1 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Peel and chop onion and carrot, chop bacon
  2. Hit oil in a pan, fry onion, carrot and bacon until bacon changes colour.
  3. Add tomatoes and mince
  4. Simmer gently until the carrots is soft and the mince is cooked.
  5. Stir the mixture occasionally
  6. Serve with cooked pasta

Chilli Con Carne

Ingredients:

500g mince 1 Onion
500g Kidney beans 1 x 400g tin tomatoes
1 tsp chilli powder Salt and pepper

Method:

  1. Reense the Kidney beans thoroughly. Peel and chop the onion.
  2. Put mince and onion in a pan, cook until brown stirring all the time.
  3. Add remaining ingredients
